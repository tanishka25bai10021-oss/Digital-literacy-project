## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the **Digital Literacy** course. It focuses on understanding the importance of digital awareness, responsible online behavior, professional communication, and cyber safety in student life.

The project was designed around the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador**, where the aim was to build awareness and practical knowledge about digital tools, online platforms, communication etiquette, and cybercrime prevention. Through this project, I explored different digital platforms and learned how technology can be used safely, effectively, and professionally in academic as well as personal life.

---

## 🎯 Objectives of the Project
- To understand the meaning and importance of digital literacy
- To create a professional and responsible online presence
- To explore coding and collaboration platforms
- To learn proper digital communication and email etiquette
- To develop awareness about cybercrime and online safety
