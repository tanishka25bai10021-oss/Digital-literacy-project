# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
1. Share content that reflects your personality in a positive and respectful way.
2. Review your privacy settings regularly to control who can view your posts and personal details.
3. Use social media to learn, connect professionally, and stay updated with useful information.
4. Think carefully before uploading photos, opinions, or comments online.
5. Maintain polite and responsible behavior while interacting with others on digital platforms.

## Don’ts
1. Do not upload content that could damage your academic or professional image.
2. Do not react impulsively to controversial posts, arguments, or online negativity.
3. Do not accept friend or follow requests from suspicious or unknown accounts without checking.
4. Do not reveal personal or financial information on public platforms.
5. Do not forward rumors, misleading posts, or unverified news to others.
